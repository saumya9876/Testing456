Saumya Verma

Co-Founder

I am a junior attending Lynbrook High School. I love to code in Java, Python, and other languages. I am very passionate about programming, and I take a lot of interest in machine learning. My passion for teaching other children and sharing my knowledge was first ignited when I was very young. Since an early age, I have always enjoyed teaching my little sister new subjects that I had learned. In my freshman year, I was able to take my passion to the next level by instructing elementary school students different topics related to science and technology. Through ChiCode, I hope to be able to reach out to more people across the world and present them with the opportunity to learn programming. I am thrilled to be one of the co-founders at ChiCode, and I look forward to making an impact through my contribution.
