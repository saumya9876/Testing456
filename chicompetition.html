About

At ChiCode, we believe that it is important to motivate students by reminding them of their accomplishments. ChiCode has come up with a competition that will run through out the course. A few students who show exceptional achievements will be rewarded with cool prizes. ChiCode has implemented a point system called ChiCompetition to determine who will be rewarded with these prizes.

Point System

Registration: 50 points

Quizzes: 45 points

Recommending ChiCode to another student: 10 points

The first few students who are able to aquire 100 points will receive prizes.
